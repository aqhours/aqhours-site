# AQHOURS — Homepage Rebuild

## Working method

- The former homepage layout is intentionally removed.
- Decide, build, and review one screen at a time. Do not add a later screen before the current one is approved.
- Do not pre-commit the homepage to an album, portfolio, resume, or product-page metaphor. The final structure should emerge from the approved screens.
- Keep visible copy sparse. Avoid product-marketing language and invented labels.

## Guardrails

- Preserve light and dark theme behavior.
- Use only facts supplied by the owner. Do not invent projects, achievements, dates, slogans, labels, or biography.
- Glass should be an intentional visual material, not a default card treatment.
- Motion must be refined and restrained, respect `prefers-reduced-motion`, and serve the composition.
- Do not copy third-party names, logos, copy, proprietary assets, or a reference site's exact composition.
